Open Data

Your final project, which I talked briefly about in the first class, is to pair a large dataset with visualizations created using D3's enter/update/exit mechanic.

Companies, governments, and citizens upload large datasets onto the web for anyone to analyze, and for us, visualize.

Homework

Create a presentation pitching your final project.

  • Find an open dataset that interests you and has at least 1,000 data points.
  • Outline the different variables available in the dataset.
  • Sketch/describe some ideas on how you could visualize that data.
  • Post your pitch on your GitHub website and prepare to present it next week.
